Common Desktop Issues

Desktops, despite their durability, face a variety of technical challenges over time. Some of the most common issues include:

  • Hardware failures: malfunctioning hard drives, RAM errors, and power supply problems.
  • Peripheral issues: keyboard, mouse, monitor, or connectivity problems.
  • Overheating and cooling system failures: often caused by dust accumulation or failing fans.
  • Software glitches: operating system errors, driver conflicts, and malware infections.
  • Network and connectivity problems: affecting both wired and wireless setups.

Each of these issues requires expert attention to prevent further damage and ensure optimal system performance.
